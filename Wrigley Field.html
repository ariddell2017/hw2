My Favorite Moments at Wrigley Field

Wrigley Field Overview

Wrigley Field is a 101-year old baseball stadium that is home to the Chicago Cubs. Located on Chicago's northside, Wrigley Field is located in the Lakeview community, and is surrounded by bars, restaurants, and residential homes. It is truly a special place, with an iconic scoreboard, outfield ivy, and marquee at the main entrance at Clark and Addison. This is a great place to visit for people from out of town, or just to bring friends if you are from Chicago. Special times lie ahead, as the team made the playoffs last year - beating the Cardinals in the NLDS.

The Sandberg Game

In a 1984 game against their long-time Cardninal rivals, Ryne Sandberg made a name for himself on national TV. Sandberg hit two game-tying homeruns in a packed house at Wrigley Field. Harry Carry's call adds to the the mystique of the moment. "He did it again, He did it again. Holy Cow! Everybody's going bananas!" From that day forward, "Ryno" was the guy on the northside!
